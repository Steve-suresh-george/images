What maintenance is required for solar panels? +
Minimal cleaning and occasional inspection are required for optimal performance.
Can I run my home completely on solar power? +
Yes, with enough panels and a battery backup system, it is possible.
What is the lifespan of solar panels? +
Most solar panels last 25 to 30 years with proper maintenance.
How long does it take to install a solar system? +
It typically takes 1 to 3 days depending on system size and site conditions.
What is the cost of installing solar panels at home? +
Costs vary, but government incentives can significantly reduce the price.
How much can I save by switching to solar? +
Savings depend on your location, usage, and available incentives.
What happens during a power outage? +
Without battery storage, most grid-tied systems will shut off during outages.
